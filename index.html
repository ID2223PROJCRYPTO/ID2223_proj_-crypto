Experimental Results

This section presents the quantitative and qualitative results of the binary classification model for predicting the 24-hour price direction of Bitcoin.

Confusion Matrix

Figure 1: Confusion matrix of the BTC price direction classifier.

The confusion matrix shows a clear dominance along the diagonal, indicating that the model captures non-random predictive signals. Misclassifications mainly occur during periods of high market volatility.

Figure 3: Comparison between predicted and actual price direction over time.
